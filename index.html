How to Play:

  • Players take turns selecting a grid cell.
  • If the selected cell follows the secret pattern, the player earns a point.
  • The turn passes to the next player regardless of move validity.
  • After each player completes one turn, the "Guess Rule" button appears on their next turn.
  • The player can try to guess the secret rule from the options shown.
  • If the guess is correct, the player wins the game.
  • If the guess is wrong, the player is eliminated.
  • If all players give up, the secret rule will be revealed.
  • Click "Restart Game" to play a new round.
