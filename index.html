How to Play:

  • Players take turns selecting a grid cell.
  • If the selected cell follows the secret pattern, the next number is placed.
  • No points are awarded for placing numbers.
  • After any move (valid or invalid), the "Guess Rule" button appears.
  • The player can guess the secret rule from the options shown.
  • If the guess is correct, the player scores points and wins.
  • If the guess is wrong, the AI must provide a counterexample.
  • The player is not eliminated and may continue to guess in future turns.
  • The game ends when the number 20 is placed, no moves remain, or all players give up.
